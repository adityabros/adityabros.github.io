This application was designed so that people can count their Hare Krishna Maha Mantra chants. Sometimes people may not have their beads or some other issue so in that case they can use this Hare Krishna maha mantra counter to fill the gap and chant and be blissful.

Why Chant this Mantra ?

It is written in the vedas that for Kali Yuga Hare Krishna Maha Mantra is the easiest and simplest method to achieve self realization. It destroys all the evil effects of Kaliyuga which are lust,greed,anger, etc all negative qualities that distances us from god. So chanting this mantra will help us be in association with God.

Ref. - Kali Santaran Upanishad.
